How are points valued?
Cash Value (1¢/pt)

Every point is worth exactly 1¢. This is what you get if you redeem points as a statement credit, direct deposit, or gift card. Simple and guaranteed.

Transfer Value (varies by currency)

Points can be transferred to airline and hotel partners (like Hyatt, United, Delta) where they're often worth more. Transfer valuations are based on industry-standard estimates:

Chase Ultimate Rewards1.5¢/pt
Amex Membership Rewards1.5¢/pt
Citi ThankYou Points1.5¢/pt
Bilt Points1.5¢/pt
Capital One Miles1¢/mi
All other currencies1¢/pt

Transfer values are estimates. Actual value depends on how and when you redeem.
